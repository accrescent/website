Download

Accrescent currently runs on Android 10 and up.

Download Accrescent

Accrescent can also be downloaded from the GrapheneOS App Store. This is the recommended approach for GrapheneOS users.

Already installed?

If you've already installed Accrescent and are still seeing this page, you've encountered a known bug in the Android Open Source Project. To work around it, go to "Settings > Apps > Accrescent > Open by default" and press "Add link." You can then check "accrescent.app" and click "Add" to manually add the link.
