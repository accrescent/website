Donate

Accrescent is sustained by donations from generous users like you. All donations go directly back into Accrescent in the form of infrastructure payments, development work, and legal fee payments. Below is a list of options you can choose from when donating.

GitHub Sponsors (recommended)

You can make a recurring or one-time donation with a credit card through GitHub Sponsors.
